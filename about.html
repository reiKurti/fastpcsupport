About FastPcSupport

Our mission is to provide patient computer help and friendly tech support tailored to seniors and those new to technology.

Founded in 2020, FastPcSupport began as a neighborhood service to help seniors and beginners overcome everyday tech frustrations. Today, our team of certified support specialists provides trusted computer help nationwide.

We believe that technology should be accessible to everyone. Our team of patient and tech-savvy support agents ensure you're never alone with your device issues.

From forgotten passwords to Zoom tutorials, we’ve helped hundreds of seniors feel confident using their computers again.
